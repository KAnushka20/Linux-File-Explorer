Wipro Limited is a leading Indian multinational corporation headquartered in Bangalore, specializing in information technology (IT) services, consulting, and business process outsourcing. Founded in 1945 by Azim Premji, it has grown into one of the world's largest IT companies, serving clients across industries with solutions in digital transformation, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and more. Known for its commitment to innovation and sustainability, Wipro employs over 250,000 people globally and is recognized for its philanthropic efforts through the Azim Premji Foundation.
